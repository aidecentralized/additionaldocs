Ambassador Application

Interested in becoming a NANDA Ambassador? We're looking for passionate advocates who can help grow the community and promote the adoption of decentralized AI agent technology.

Ambassador Responsibilities

  • Organize local meetups and educational events (virtual or in-person)
  • Create content such as blog posts, tutorials, or videos about NANDA
  • Represent NANDA at industry events and academic conferences
  • Support new community members and facilitate connections
  • Provide feedback on protocol development and roadmap

Ambassador Benefits

  • Early access to new protocol features and research
  • Dedicated mentorship from core team members
  • Speaking opportunities at NANDA events
  • Event support and promotional materials
  • Recognition on the NANDA website and official channels

Terms and Conditions

The NANDA Ambassador Program is governed by the following terms and conditions. Please read carefully before applying.

Term Length

Ambassador appointments are for a renewable 12-month term. Continued participation is contingent upon active engagement with the program and adherence to these terms.

Expectations

  • Organize at least one community event per quarter (online or in-person)
  • Create a minimum of one piece of content about NANDA every two months
  • Participate in monthly ambassador calls
  • Provide regular updates on local community activities
  • Adhere to the NANDA Code of Conduct in all community interactions
  • Accurately represent NANDA's technology and mission

Responsibilities

  • Build and nurture local communities interested in decentralized AI
  • Provide feedback to the core team on community needs and challenges
  • Connect potential contributors and partners with the NANDA ecosystem
  • Stay informed about protocol developments and roadmap

Benefits

  • Recognition on the NANDA website with profile and contact information
  • Access to private Discord channels and ambassador resources
  • Early access to new protocol features and documentation
  • Exclusive ambassador swag and digital badges
  • Reimbursement for approved community event expenses (subject to limits)
  • Opportunity to shape the future of decentralized AI agent technologies

Note: The NANDA Ambassador Program is a volunteer position. While we provide support and resources, ambassadors are not employees or contractors of the NANDA organization.
